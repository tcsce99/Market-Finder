- [ ] Get on a quick call, walk them through the 3 fixes

## Phase 4, Deliver the work (per client)
- [ ] Month 1 setup: fix category, add services, description, service areas, photos, working web link, review requests, reply to old reviews
- [ ] Monthly: 1 post/week, fresh photos, reply to reviews, keep info current, send a 1-page monthly report
- [ ] Use AI (me) to draft the description, posts, and review replies

## Phase 5, Grow
- [ ] Repeat outreach until you hit 10 to 17 clients ($3,000 to $5,000/mo)
- [ ] Ask happy clients for referrals (contractors know contractors)
- [ ] Optional later: a simple one-page Surfaced HTX website (Carrd or Google Site) for credibility

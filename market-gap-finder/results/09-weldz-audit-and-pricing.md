# PART B — Pricing & offer (read before you send)

## Recommended offer (simple, low-friction, gets you to $3–5k)

**$300/month · month-to-month · cancel anytime · no setup fee (first 3 clients).**

Say it exactly like this on the call:
> "It's $300 a month, month-to-month — no contract, cancel anytime. I'll waive the usual setup fee since you'd be one of my first Houston clients. If you're not seeing more calls in 60 days, cancel and we're square."

### Why this structure

- **$300/mo is trivial for them to justify.** Fence/welding jobs run $2,000–8,000. One extra job *a year* pays for ~2 years of your fee. You never have to argue ROI — the math is absurd in your favor. Don't undercharge out of nerves.
- **Month-to-month, no contract** kills the #1 objection ("what if it doesn't work"). You don't need a contract to keep clients — results keep them.
- **Waive setup for your first 3.** Right now you need *case studies and testimonials* more than a $200 setup fee. Waiving it removes the last hesitation and gets your proof faster. (Charge a $200–300 setup once you have 2–3 wins to point to.)
- **The 60-day soft guarantee** is your closer. It costs you little (you're confident it works) and flips the risk onto you, which is exactly what makes a cautious owner say yes.

### The math to your goal
| Clients | Monthly | Time (≈45 min/client/mo once set up) |
|---|---|---|
| 10 | **$3,000** | ~7.5 hrs/mo |
| 14 | **$4,200** | ~10.5 hrs/mo |
| 17 | **$5,100** | ~13 hrs/mo |

**10–17 recurring clients = your entire $3–5k goal**, at a few hours a week of upkeep once each is set up. That's the whole business on one page. Landing them is the work; keeping them is easy.

### Scope boundary — be honest (fits your no-code reality)
- **What you sell:** Google Business Profile setup + optimization + monthly management + reviews. That's checklist work AI helps you do — no coding.
- **The "website" fix:** don't promise custom web development (not your skill). For a dead/missing site, either (a) point their listing to a **free Google Business "website"** (built into GBP, no code), or (b) stand up a **one-page Carrd site (~$19/yr)** — light, no maintenance. Frame it as "get a working link on your listing," not "I'll build you a website." Keep the boundary clean so you never sell something you can't maintain.
- **Don't** take on anything requiring ongoing code. If a client wants a real custom site, refer it out.

### Optional add-ons (later, once you have wins)
- Review-generation as a standalone: +$100–150/mo.
- One-time GBP setup/cleanup for people who won't do monthly: $250–400 flat.
